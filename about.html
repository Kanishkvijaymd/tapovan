About Us

At Tapovan Hostels, we provide a safe, comfortable, and study oriented living environment designed to meet the unique needs of students preparing for competetive entrance exams, ensuring a perfect balance of homely warmth and modern amenities so that our resident students can focus on thier study goals.

The hostel is managed by owners who stay at the same hostel so that personal attention is given to the needs of each and every student staying.

Mr Bhupendra Malhotra (General Manager) has more than 35 years of experience in coaching industry, Handling the requirements and understanding the needs of students since 1990s.

Mrs Anjana Malhotra (Chief Warden) has more than 15 years of experience in nurturing students for competetive exams. With a passion in cooking she ensures that each student eats a perfect meal and feels safe as home at the hostel.
